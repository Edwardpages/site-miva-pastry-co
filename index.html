About Miva

Founded in Austin, TX, Miva Pastry Co brings warmth and craftsmanship to every bake. Miva started this journey with a simple belief: that food made with care tastes better.

Each morning, we hand-mix, shape, and bake our goods using only the finest ingredients. From sourdough starters nurtured for years to buttery croissant layers folded by hand, we take pride in every detail.

We're more than a bakery — we're a community space where neighbors gather for fresh bread, sweet treats, and heartfelt moments.
